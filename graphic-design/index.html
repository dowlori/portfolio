Adding Your Work

Add images to graphic-design/assets/ in your repo, then reference them with an <img> tag inside each .gallery-img-inner div. Use class="tall" or class="wide" on a card to vary the grid layout. Compress images before uploading using Squoosh — aim for under 300KB per image.
